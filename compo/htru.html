 Ranchi by Air

Ranchi’s domestic airport – Birsa Munda Airport (Code: IXR) is situated approximately 7 km south of the institute. It is well connected with other metro cities like Kolkata, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune and Chennai.

There are eight direct daily flights to Ranchi from Mumbai, New Delhi and Kolkata at convenient timings.
